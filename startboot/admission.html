Admission Requirement

Necessary documents (originals)

  • Certificate of completed intermediate education
  • Reference of Ministry of Education of the UNIVERSITY on equivalency to secondary education of the UNIVERSITY
  • Passport with appropriate entry visa
  • Medical reference – 086 form, (medical inspection can be provided in the Medical Centre )
  • 8 photos (3x4)

Necessary documents when being transferred from other Higher Medical Institution

  • Certificate of completed intermediate education
  • Reference of Ministry of education of the UNIVERSITY on equivalency to secondary education of the UNIVERSITY
  • Academic reference from previous Higher Medical Institution
  • Certificate of Preparatory department
  • Passport, with appropriate entry visa
  • Medical reference-086 form (medical inspection can be provided in the Medical Centre )
  • 8 photographs (3x4)

How to apply

First STEP –

  • Required document for admission
  • Std. 10th mark sheet with certificate
  • Std .11th mark sheet
  • Std . 12th mark sheet with certificate

Second STEP –

Required document for passport

  • Birth certificate
  • Voter id
  • Residence proof
  • Photo colour 8 passport size

THIRD STEP –

  • Medical report
  • All document legalization and translate from HRD & embassy
  • Invitation
  • Visa
  • Air ticket
  • Miscellaneous expenses ( only for the first year ) university fees

FORTH STEP –

  • After you have arrived at abroad airport and university
  • Your passport shall be registered by ministry of interior of that country embassy within 5 days from the date of arrival.
  • Excluding holidays and weekends
  • Assistance in registration and visa extension will be provided foreign student must get registered between 6 month and 1 year
  • Passport must be presented to the department of international affairs, 1 month before the expiration of visa
  • Failure to meet registration and visa terms results in fine, set by ministry of interior of that country

DEADLINE FOR ADMISSION OF INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS DEPENDS ON THE SELECTED PROGRAM

- Fall semester – until 5th September

THE ADDITIONAL CHARGES OF INVITION, VISA , ETC. ARE PAYABLE AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION . THE DETAILS ARE AVAILABLE WITH OUR INDIAN ASSOSIATES .

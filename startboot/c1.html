About BATUMI SHOTA RUSTAVELI STATE UNIVERSITY

Batumi University is the oldest and most recognized university in the entire region having a state board of 125 years old history. In 2009 the LEPL Batumi Botanical Garden was joined to the University and Niko Berdgen ishvili Research Institute of Photo pathology were constituted. In 2011 a Scientific Centre was established comprising 5 faculties: Education and Sciences, Social Sciences, Business and Law, Agrarian Technologies an Ecology , Tourism and Engineering, Technologies. At present, about 5000 students study at the vocational, Bachelor, Master and Doctoral programs. At present Teaching and Research processes involve 244 professors, 55 researchers and 387 visiting professors.

The University actively engaged into directions of Natural sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, Law, Mathematical and Computer Sciences, Engineering Technologies, Business and Public Health as well as Agricultural fields . Students studying here have opportunity to work as a junior resident doctor in pedagogical scientific activities and in Medical theory.

Education Base

Shota Rostaveli University is oriented towards the demands of the society and the state. Its first and foremost mission is:

To offer high quality education to all the interested people despite Nationality, religion and political status. To educate highly qualified, competitive and adaptive specialists to the changing professional environment, with the competencies relevant to the modern requirements, who will be the free personalities with high civic understanding and activity, bearers of the principles of humanism, democracy and liberal values. To accomplish fundamental and applied research in natural sciences, humanities, social sciences ,public health for the purpose of welfare of the population, economic strength, defence ability establishment of democratic principles and raising spirituality.

Amenities

University provides accommodation in hostels with all modern facilities. Hostels have access to internet services and students are protected by the Association of students. The mandatory requirement for staying in the hostel is strict maintenance of internal rules of the hostel. A laundry and press service also operates. Special workers have been appointed in order to maintain cleanliness in the hostel rooms, corridors, premises, toilets and bathrooms.

A group of Two or three students are allotted a room. This room is furnished with beds, side tables, chairs, one large sized study table, and a cupboard. Pillows, Bed Sheets, Mattresses, Quilts and Blankets are provided by the hostel in charge. Each room has a large window for easy ventilation. Every room has an automated heating device that runs 24x7 during the winter snow season to maintain optimum temperature in the hostel rooms. Each hostel floor has a set of toilets and bathrooms that have a regular 24x7 cold and hot water supply.

International Co-operation

International Relations Department is a supporting structural unit of LEPL Batumi Shota Rustaveli State University.

The department deals with the establishment of relations with foreign universities and relevant scientific-research institutions and educational centres. It has to spread the information about the exchange programs and put them into practice. The Department is also in charge of the minutes (protocols); arrangement of hosting the delegations of foreign universities and organizations; development of collaboration with the higher educational institutions of the countries involved in Bologna Process; serving foreign students; supporting mobility process of students and other relevant services.
